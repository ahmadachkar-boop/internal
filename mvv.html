Mission, Vision & Values

Who we are, what we do, and how we do it — for Aggieland.

Mission

CARPOOL will facilitate a safe and reliable commuting environment in the Bryan/College Station area by providing free, non-judgmental rides home to intoxicated patrons and/or patrons rendered incapable of transportation due to other circumstances.

Vision

CARPOOL strives to become the most effective and sought-after designated driver program in the nation through our endless dedication, unique operations, commitment to the safety of our fellow students, focus on values, and the constant improvement of our volunteers.

Core Values

✓

We take ownership of CARPOOL's ideals, fulfill our responsibilities, and uphold the reputation we work to achieve.

✓

We unconditionally respect ourselves, patrons, and others through our thoughts, words, and actions.

✓

While community safety is our driving concern, the safety of our members is our top priority.

✓

We promote equality and embrace diversity as a foundation of our organization and an asset to the community.

✓

Teamwork and communication unite us to accomplish our goals; we work to eliminate division while maintaining a fun atmosphere.

✓

Through constant evaluation, innovation, and improvement, we pursue success and the development of our program and ourselves.

✓

As members of CARPOOL we hold ourselves and each other accountable for upholding these values.
